This is assuming you already have Epic's ART rigging tools installed in maya.

...Epic Games\4.11\Engine\Extras\Maya_AnimationRiggingTools\MayaTools\General\ART\Projects\

...Epic Games\4.11\Engine\Extras\Maya_AnimationRiggingTools\MayaTools\General\Icons\ART\Thumbnails\
